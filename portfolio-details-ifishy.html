Ifishy

🌊🐟 Struggling with Fish Health Problems? Meet iFishy, Your Aquaculture Superhero! 🦸‍♂️

🔎 The Problem:
Fish diseases can appear out of nowhere, leaving fish farmers and ornamental fish entrepreneurs scrambling for answers. Lack of expertise means:

  • ❌ Delayed disease detection.
  • ❌ Costly treatments.
  • ❌ Devastating losses in production or prized ornamental fish.

💡 The Solution? iFishy!
Your smartphone just became a fish health expert. With iFishy, simply snap a photo of your fish, and our AI-powered app will:

  • ✅ Diagnose diseases in seconds.
  • ✅ Provide step-by-step treatment recommendations.
  • ✅ Help you save time, money, and your fish!

🎯 Powered by cutting-edge AI and designed for ease of use, iFishy is here to revolutionize fish farming and aquaculture. Whether you're a fish farmer or an ornamental fish enthusiast, iFishy is your trusted partner for healthier fish and thriving businesses.

💼 This innovative app is brought to you by Team C242-PS164 from Bangkit Academy, an industry-driven program by Google, Tokopedia, Gojek, and Traveloka. We're proud to leverage technology to solve real-world problems and make a difference in aquaculture!

🚀 Don’t let fish diseases sink your success. Download iFishy and dive into a smarter way to care for your fish today! 🌟🐟
