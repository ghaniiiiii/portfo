Internship Portal

Welcome to the Internship Portal, a platform that connects students with companies for the best internship opportunities!

🌟 Main Features:

  • ✅ Authentication: Secure login and registration.
  • ✅ Role Management: Support for Admin, Company, and Student roles.
  • ✅ Dynamic Dashboard: Access features tailored to user roles.
  • ✅ Job Post Management: Companies can post, edit, and delete internship listings.
  • ✅ Internship Search: Students can browse and apply for internship positions.

🛠️ Technologies Used:

  • 💻 Laravel Framework
  • 📊 MySQL for database management
  • 🔒 Middleware for authentication and role management
  • 🌐 Blade Templating for responsive UI

🚀 How It Works:
After registration, users can log in based on their assigned roles:

  • Admin: Manage users, roles, and internship data.
  • Company: Post job listings and review applicants.
  • Student: Browse job listings, apply for internships, and manage their applications.

🎯 Objective:
To provide a practical solution that simplifies the internship search for students while helping companies find the best talent.
